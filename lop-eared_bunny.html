Pig Plush Amigurumi Pattern



We can't deny the fact that there is something about bunnies that we can't resist. Maybe it's their cuteness?

Crochet it today with our step-by-step Lop-Eared Bunny Pattern!



Materials:
  • Chunky yarn
  • 4 ply yarn
  • 4 mm crochet hook
  • 8 mm safety eyes
  • Fiberfill
  • Black and white embroidery floss
  • Sewing needle and scissors
Abbreviations:

ch = chain

st = stitch

sc = single crochet

inc = increase (2 sc in 1 st)

dec = decrease (2 sc together)

sl st = slip stitch

dc = double crochet

tr = treble crochet

(..) = repeat the instructions in brackets based on the given no. of times

[..] = total number of stitches



Arms (make 2):

Crochet with gray yarn.

Rnd 1: sc x6 in maginc ring [6]

Rnd 2: inc x6 [12]

Rnd 3-4: sc x12 [12]

Rnd 5: (sc x2, dec) x3 [9]

Rnd 6-12: sc x9 [9]

Stuff only hands. Flatten the edges and crochet 4 sc through both layers, closing the hall. Fasten off.



Legs (make 2):

Rnd 1: sc x6 in maginc ring [6]

Rnd 2: inc x6 [12]

Rnd 3: (sc, inc) x6 [18]

Rnd 4-6: sc x18 [18]

Rnd 7: dec x3, sc x12 [15]

Rnd 8: dec x3, sc x9 [12]

Rnd 9-12: sc x12 [12]

Stuff only half of the legs.

Rnd 13: sc x11, flatten the edges and crochet 6 sc through both layers, closing the hole. [12]

Fasten off.



Head and Body:

Rnd 1: sc x6 in magic ring [6]

Rnd 2: inc x6 [12]

Rnd 3: (sc, inc) x6 [18]

Rnd 4: (sc x2, inc) x6 [24]

Rnd 5: (sc x3, inc) x6 [30]

Rnd 6: (sc x4, inc) x6 [36]

Attach legs while working on the next round.

Rnd 7: sc x6, sc x6 crocheting through the st of both leg and body, sc x12 of the body, sc x6 of both the other leg and the body, sc x6 of the body [36]

Rnd 8-12: sc x36 [36]

Rnd 13: sc x12, dec x6, sc x12 [30]

Rnd 14: (sc x3, dec) x6 [24]

Rnd 15: sc x24 [24]

Rnd 17: attach arms while working on this round in the same way as you attached legs [18]

Begin working on the head.

Rnd 18: inc x18 [36]

Rnd 19: (sc x5, inc) x6 [42]

Rnd 20-24: sc x42 [42]

Rnd 25: (sc x5, dec) x6 [36]

Rnd 26: (sc x4, dec) x6 [30]

Rnd 27: (sc x3, dec) x6 [24]

Rnd 28: sc x24 [24]

Insert safety eyes in the head between Rnds 24 and 25 (about 6 st in between the eyes).

Rnd 29: (sc x2, dec) x6 [18]

Rnd 30: (sc, dec) x6 [12]

Rnd 31: dec x6 [6]

p>Fasten off leaving a smal tail.



Ears (make 2):

Work with gray yarn.

Crochet 21 ch. Continue working beginnig from 2nd ch from the hook.

Rnd 1: sc x7, hdc x6, dc x6, 3 dc in last st, dc x5, hdc x6, sc x7 [41]

Turn and crochet in opposite direction.

Rnd 2: ch 1, sc x19, inc x3, sc x19 [44]

Crochet 4 sc along the edge of the ear.

Fasten off.



Tail:

Rnd 1: sc x6 in magic ring [6]

Rnd 2: inc x6 [12]

Rnd 3: (sc x2, dec) x3 [9]

Finish with sl st. Fasten off leaving a tail for sewing. Stuff a little bit with fiberfill.



Bow:

Use yellow yarn.

Rnd 1: (4 ch, 3 tr, 4 ch, sl st) x2

Fasten off. Wrap the middle part of the bow with yarn tail several times and tie a knot.



Assembly:
  1. Pin ears to the head and sew them on Rnd 28.
  2. Sew tail on Rnds 5-7 at the back.
  3. Embroider a nose and a line under nouse with black cotton yarn.
  4. Embroider eyebrows, eye lines and eye whites. When making eye whites and eye lines, pull thread slightly to get indents.
  5. Sew the bow to the head, near one of the ears.
