Pig Plush Amigurumi Pattern



This adorable pig is too sweet like for words like its color. Alongside with its chonky body is a carrot-made sling bag.

Crochet it today with our step-by-step Pig Plush Pattern!



Materials:
  • Chunky yarn
  • 4 ply yarn
  • 7 mm crochet hook
  • 8 mm safety eyes
  • Fiberfill
  • Black embroidery floss
  • Sewing needle and scissors
Abbreviations:

ch = chain

st = stitch

sc = single crochet

inc = increase (2 sc in 1 st)

dec = decrease (2 sc together)

sl st = slip stitch

dc = double crochet

tr = treble crochet

(..) = repeat the instructions in brackets based on the given no. of times

[..] = total number of stitches



Head:

Crochet with tan pink yarn.

ch 6 + 1

Rnd 1: sc x5 on the last chain then sc 5 on the other side of ch [12]

Rnd 2: (sc, inc) x6 [18]

Rnd 3: (sc x2, inc) x6 [24]

Rnd 4: (sc x3, inc) x6 [30]

Rnd 5: (sc x4, inc) x6 [36]

Rnd 6-9: sc x36 [36]

Insert the eyes on Rnd 8 and make them 6 st apart.

Rnd 10: (sc x4, dec) x6 [30]

Rnd 11: (sc x3, dec) [24]

Rnd 14: (sc x2, dec) x6 [18]

Rnd 15: (sc, dec) x6 [12]

Fasten off. There's no need to close off the remaining hole since it will be covered later.



Ears (make 2):

ch 2 + 1

Rnd 1: sc, hdc [2]



Body:

Rnd 1: sc x6 in magic ring [6]

Rnd 2: inc x6 [12]

Rnd 3: (sc, inc) x6 [18]

Rnd 4: (sc x2, inc) x6 [24]

Rnd 5-8:sc x24 [24]

Rnd 9: (sc x4, dec) x6 [20]

Fasten off leaving a small tail.



Arms (make 2):

Work with yellow yarn.

Rnd 1: sc x4 in magic ring [4]

Rnd 2-3: sc x4 [4]

Fasten off



Legs:

Rnd 1: sc x4 in magic ring [4]

Rnd 2: sc x4 [4]

Fasten off



Snout:

ch 4 + 1

Rnd 1: sc x3, inc in the last ch, sc x3 on the other side of chain [8]

Fasten off



Carrot Pouch (optional):

Start with orange yarn.

Rnd 1: sc x6 in magic ring [6]

Rnd 2: sc x6 [6]

Rnd 3: (sc x2, inc) x6 [8]

Rnd 4: (sc, inc) x6 [12]

Rnd 5: ch 47 and sl st in the 7th st from where the chain starts [8]

Fasten off



Use green yarn.

Rnd 1: sl st in tge stitch next to the one where you ended with the orange yarn

Rnd 2: ch 3, sc 2, and sl st in the next st.

Repeat until you reach the other side of the chain or until you have 5 leaves.

Fasten off and weave in the tail ends.



Assembly:
  1. Start by sewing the head to thebody, leaving 4 rnds beneath the eyes.
  2. Sew the arms to the sides of thebody, on the round you finished off the body with
  3. Sew the ears to the top of the head, between Rnds 3 and 4.
  4. Sew the legs between Rnds 4 and 5 of the body. Sew though the back loops only.

    5. You may attach the snout by sewing through the back loops only or by sewing through the back of the stitches. Here is what we recommend:

  5. First, pin the snout onto the head so that there is an equal distance between both eyes.
  6. Sew behind the sts, not through them
  7. Sewing this way will keep a sharp oval shape for the snout.
  8. Using black yarn, sew two small lines onto the snout
