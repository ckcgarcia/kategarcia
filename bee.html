Bee Amigurumi Pattern



Here is un-BEE-lievably cute bee that you can make. It became famous in TikTok and now you can make your own Bee!

Crochet it today with our step-by-step Pig Plush Pattern!



Materials:
  • Chunky yarn
  • 4 ply yarn
  • 7 mm crochet hook
  • 8 mm safety eyes
  • Fiberfill
  • Black embroidery floss
  • Sewing needle and scissors
Abbreviations:

ch = chain

st = stitch

sc = single crochet

inc = increase (2 sc in 1 st)

dec = decrease (2 sc together)

sl st = slip stitch

dc = double crochet

tr = treble crochet

(..) = repeat the instructions in brackets based on the given no. of times

[..] = total number of stitches



Head:

Crochet with yellow yarn.

Rnd 1: sc x6 in magic circle [6]

Rnd 2: ch 1, inc x6, sl st [12]

Rnd 3: ch 1, (sc, inc) x6, sl st [18]

Rnd 4: ch 1, (sc, sc, inc) x6 [24]

Rnd 5-6: ch 1, sc x24 [24]



Switch to black yarn

Rnd 7-8: (sc x4, dec) x6 [30]



Switch to yellow yarn

Rnd 9-10: (sc x4, dec) x6 [30]



Switch to black yarn

Rnd 11-12: (sc x4, dec) x6 [30]



Attach the safety eyes (12 mm). Place it between Rnds 3 and 4.



Switch to yellow yarn

Rnd 13-14: (sc x4, dec) x6 [24]



Switch to black yarn

Rnd 15: ch 1, (sc x2, dec), sl st x6 [18]



Stuff the bee body.

Rnd 16: ch 1, (sc, dec) x6, sl st [18]



Switch to yellow yarn

Rnd 17: ch 1, (dec) x6, sl st [24]



Fasten off leaving a tail and use the tail to sew the remaining hole closed.



Wings (make 2):

Using white yarn.

Rnd 1: sc x6 in magic ring [6]

Rnd 2: ch 1, 2 hdc x6, sl st [12]

Fasten off leaving a long tail and sew it onto the bee.
