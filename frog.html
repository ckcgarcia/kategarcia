Frog Amigurumi Pattern



Frog seems to be getting popular these days. Just look at their adorable faces! Crochet it today with our step-by-step Frog Amigurumi Pattern!

Crochet it today with our step-by-step Tiny Lion Amigurumi Pattern!



Materials:
  • 4 ply yarn
  • 2.00 mm crochet hook
  • 8 mm safety eyes
  • Fiberfill
  • Black embroidery floss
  • Sewing needle and scissors
Abbreviations:

ch = chain

st = stitch

sc = single crochet

inc = increase (2 sc in 1 st)

dec = decrease (2 sc together)

sl st = slip stitch

dc = double crochet

tr = treble crochet

(..) = repeat the instructions in brackets based on the given no. of times

[..] = total number of stitches



Head and body:

Crochet with green yarn.

Rnd 1: sc x6 in magic ring [6]

Rnd 2: (inc) x6 [12]

Rnd 3: (sc, inc) x6 [18]

Rnd 4: (sc x2, inc) x6 [24]

Rnd 5: (sc x3, inc) x6 [30]

Rnd 6: (sc x4, inc) x6 [36]

Rnd 7: (sc x5, inc) x6 [42]

Rnd 8-15: sc x42 [42]

Rnd 16: (sc x5, dec) x6 [36]

Rnd 17: (sc x4, dec) x6 [36]

Rnd 18: (sc x3, dec) x6 [24]

Rnd 19: (sc x2, dec) x6 [18]

Rnd 20: (sc, dec) x6 [12]

Rnd 21: (dec) x6 [6]

Fasten off and weave in the ends.



Eyes (make 2):

Crochet with green yarn.

Rnd 1: sc x12 [12]

Rnd 2: (inc x2, sc) x2 [10]

Rnd 3-5: sc x10 [10]

Stuff the eyes. If using safety eyes, insert them between Rnd 4 and Rnd 5. Fasten off leaving a long tail for sewing.



Arms (make 2):

Rnd 1: sc x6 in magic ring [6]

Rnd 2: sc x6 [6]

Rnd 3: (sc x2, inc) x2 [8]

Rnd 4: sc x8 [8]

Stuff.

Don't stuff and sc through both the layers at the top of the arms to close. Fasten off leaving a long tail for sewing.



Legs (make 2):

Rnd 1: sc x6 in magic ring [6]

Rnd 2: sc x2 [12]

Rnd 3-5: sc x12 [12]

Stuff the legs and fasten off.



Belly Patch:

Work with white yarn.

Rnd 1: sc x6 in magic ring [6]

Rnd 2: inc x6 [12]

Rnd 3: (sc, inc) x6 [18]

Rnd 4: (sc, inc, sc) x6 [24]

Rnd 5: (sc x3, inc) x6 [30]

Rnd 6: (sc x2, inc, sc x2) x6 [36]

Fasten off leaving a tail for sewing.



Assembly:
  1. Sew the eyes between R5 -7 on either side of the head.
  2. If using flat back eyes or felt, glue the eyes at this point.
  3. Sew the belly patch to the body just below R7.
  4. Attach arms a little slantingly between R10 -12.
  5. Sew the legs around R15- 17.
  6. Embroider the mouth using black thread.
  7. Cut 2 small round shaped pink felt for the cheeks and glue it below the eyes.
